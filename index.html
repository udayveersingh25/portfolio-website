About Me

I’ve always been drawn to the mysteries of the universe. Back in high school, astrophysics fascinated me — the math, the cosmic scale, the idea of understanding existence through equations. That passion led me to prepare for JEE, aiming for a good college where I could dive deeper into space science. But life had other plans. During my JEE prep at Allen, Chandigarh, living away from home, something new caught my attention — artificial intelligence. It started in early 2023 when ChatGPT-3.5 dropped and suddenly, the world was talking about AI. I began experimenting with everything I could find — image generators, voice models, music AIs — fascinated by how creativity and computation could merge. Over time, my curiosity shifted from stars to systems, from telescopes to algorithms. I realized that what drew me to astrophysics — discovery, pattern-seeking, understanding complex systems — was also at the heart of AI. That realization steered me toward computer science, and eventually, to the Scaler School of Technology, where I’m now building the foundation to create and understand the next generation of intelligent systems.
